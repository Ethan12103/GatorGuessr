About
Welcome to GatorGuessr! A game that tests your knowledge of the city of Gainesville. Similar to the game GeoGuessr, it places users in a random location in Gainesville, from the beautiful UF campus to Butler Plaza and everything in between. The game is played in one round, and the user gains points depending on how close they are to the actual location. This game was developed by the four of us below as a final project for our Software Engineering class. We hope you enjoy!
